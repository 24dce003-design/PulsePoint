Protein Shake (Chocolate Banana)

  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 cup milk or almond milk
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter (optional)
  • 4â€“5 ice cubes
  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Grilled Chicken Salad

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup lettuce or spinach
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil & lemon juice
  1. Grill the chicken breast and slice it.
  2. Combine all vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken on top and add dressing.
  4. Mix and serve fresh.

Oat Energy Bites (No-Bake)

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Veggie Omelette

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Whisk the eggs and mix with veggies.
  2. Cook in a pan until both sides are golden.
  3. Serve with whole-grain toast.

Quinoa & Chickpea Bowl

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil
  1. Mix quinoa, chickpeas, and veggies in a bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss well and enjoy chilled.

Peanut Butter Banana Toast

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • Optional: cinnamon, chia seeds
  1. Toast the bread slice.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly.
  3. Top with banana slices.
  4. Sprinkle with optional toppings.

Green Detox Smoothie

  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 green apple
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 cup water or coconut water
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  1. Chop and wash ingredients.
  2. Blend with liquid until smooth.
  3. Serve fresh and cold.
